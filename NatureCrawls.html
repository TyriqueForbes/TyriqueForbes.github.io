Nature Crawls

A first person arena shooter where you take the role of a legendary conservationist fighting off hoards of hostile machines aiming to destroy the little precious nature left in a far cyberpunk future. Each weapon used relates to an animal of the Big Five, all of whom are represented as anime women. The players aim is to unlock all weapons and see how many waves can be survived
